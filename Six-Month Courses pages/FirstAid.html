First Aid


Fees: R1500


Purpose: To provide first aid awareness and basic life support.

This course will allow students to confidently and carefully respond to the most common medical emergencies. Here you will learn what to do until paramedics arrive. You will be taught life-saving skills like cpr and wound treatment.


Content:

  • Wounds and bleeding

  • Burns and fractures

  • Emergency scene management

  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)

  • Respiratory distress e.g., Choking, blocked airway
