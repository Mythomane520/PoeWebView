Sewing


Fees: R1500


Purpose: To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services.

This course will allow students to confidently alter and create clothing. Here you will learn how to properly take measurements, use sewing machines, and how to follow the most common stitch patterns.


Content:

  • Types of stitches

  • Threading a sewing machine

  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems and seams

  • Alterations

  • Designing and sewing new garments
