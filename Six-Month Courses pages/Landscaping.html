Landscaping


Fees: R1500


Purpose: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens.

This course will allow students to design and create beautiful gardens in different styles. Here you will learn about local and exotic plants, what they need and how to take care of them. Lastly, the student will learn about fixtures like footpaths, statues and built-in braaiers.


Content:

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees

  • Fixed structures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)

  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden

  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours

  • Garden layout
