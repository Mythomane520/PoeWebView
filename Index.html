    • Empowering the Nation, founded by Precious Radebe,

    is dedicated to empowering domestic workers and gardeners through skills training.

    Inspired by the lack of opportunities her family faced,

    Precious saw a need in her community to uplift those in similar circumstances.

    Her training school equips participants with valuable skills,enhancing their employability

    and enabling them to command higher wages.Moreover, graduates have the potential to become entrepreneurs,

    leveraging their newfound expertise to start their own ventures.

    This initiative not only benefits individuals but also meets the demand from employers seeking skilled household services,

    creating a win-win situation for all involved.
